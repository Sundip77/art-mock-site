Early Life

Jacques Bermon Webster II, known professionally as Travis Scott, was born on April 30, 1991, in Houston, Texas. Raised in Missouri City by his parents Wanda and Jacques Webster Sr., he spent his early years with his grandmother Sealie in Houston's Sunnyside neighborhood.

Musical Roots

Travis inherited his musical talent from his father, Jacques Sr., who played piano and drums, and his grandfather, who was a jazz composer. His father bought him his first drum set at age 3, sparking his passion for music. His mother Wanda influenced his fashion sense, often dressing him in premium brands and sharing her fashion magazines.

Family Life

Scott's relationship with Kylie Jenner began in 2017, and they welcomed their daughter Stormi Webster in February 2018. The couple has since welcomed their second child, a son, in 2022. Today, Travis runs the Cactus Jack Foundation with his parents, supporting youth through education and creative resources.
